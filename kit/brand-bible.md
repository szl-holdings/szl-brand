## 4 · Voice + Tone

**Math-grounded, story-aware, never mystical, never marketing-fluff.**

We pair two registers and never let either run alone:

- **Math** gives the claim its floor: a number, a bound, a theorem name, a receipt hash.
- **Story** gives the claim its meaning: the khipu cord, the journey (*puriq* = "the one who
  walks"), the Andean lineage that names the parts.

The discipline: *every story sentence is anchored to a checkable artifact; every math sentence
is given a human reason to exist.* Mythos as decoration is banned (it is a literally banned
token, see §6). The Andean references are **engineering analogies with citations**, not ritual.

### Voice principles

1. **Claim only what is on disk.** If a number is not in the canonical counter, do not print it.
2. **Label proof status honestly.** PROVEN / SORRY / AXIOM / CONJECTURE — never collapse them.
3. **Prefer the smaller true number to the larger impressive one.** 456 source-declared is the
   honest count; 749 is the doctrine-claimed count; show both, never blur them.
4. **No superlatives without a benchmark.** "First", "best", "world-class", "revolutionary" are
   fluff unless a cited, reproducible comparison backs them — and then you cite, not assert.
5. **Cite the source, name the source.** Inline, with a real URL. Never "(source)".
6. **Quechua names get an etymology and a citation, every time** (see §5).

### Tone matrix

| Surface | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Product UI microcopy | terse, precise, calm | "Receipt verified · chain root 3f9a…" |
| Docs / README | explanatory, sourced | "Λ is the weighted geometric mean; uniqueness is open (Conjecture 1)." |
| Investor / Series-A | confident, bounded | "6 tracked sorries at the re-audited root; all tagged, none hidden." |
| Error / safety | direct, non-alarming | "Action halted: tripwire T10 (STOP) fired. No receipt was written." |

### Anti-patterns (never do this)

- "Our revolutionary mythos-driven AI is fully verified and world-class." — five violations.
- "Zero sorries." / "SLSA L3." / "100% proven." — banned overclaims (§6).
- Mystical framing of the khipu as magic rather than as a Merkle/sum-of-sums accumulator.
