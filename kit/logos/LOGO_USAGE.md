# SZL Holdings — Logo Usage

The SZL mark is one artifact at three zoom levels: a logo, a number, and a Lean theorem are
the same thing. The orbit is the receipt chain you can walk; the coral node is a single
provable claim on that orbit; the Λ is the Λ-Spine aggregator — open as **Conjecture 1**, drawn
small and honest, never overstated. We render what the anatomy proves. We do not invent.

## 3 · Clear space

Maintain a clear-space margin around the mark equal to **the cap-height of the monogram (the
height of the "S")** on all four sides. Nothing — text, rules, other logos, image edges — may
intrude into this zone. For the horizontal lockup, use the **height of the orbit** as the clear
zone above and below.

---

## 4 · Minimum size

| Asset | Minimum | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Full primary (with lockup) | **120 px** wide on screen / **32 mm** in print | Below this the "SZL HOLDINGS" lockup loses legibility. |
| Mark only (favicon) | **24 px** | The monogram stays legible; orbit/Λ drop out by design at small sizes. |
| Horizontal lockup | **180 px** wide | Keeps the right-hand wordmark readable. |

Below 24 px, use `szl_favicon_32.png` (or render `szl_favicon.svg` to the target size) — never
shrink the full lockup that small.

## 5 · Do

- Place the primary mark on the space-navy field `#030F29`, or use the transparent version on
  brand-appropriate darks.
- Keep the orbit tilt at its authored **−22°**; it encodes the orbit motif and pairs with the
  product surfaces.
- Use the **one-color** versions for single-ink print, laser/CNC, embroidery, or watermarks.
- Let the coral node be the **only** saturated color in a composition.
- Reproduce from the **SVG** whenever the medium allows (it is resolution-independent and crisp).

## 6 · Don't

- **Don't** recolor the monogram (it is white on navy, or the one-color mono versions — nothing
  else).
- **Don't** make the coral node larger, add a second node, or use coral as a fill/background.
- **Don't** rotate, skew, stretch, or condense the mark; don't change the orbit tilt.
- **Don't** add a drop shadow, glow, bevel, outline, or gradient overlay to the whole mark.
- **Don't** re-typeset the lockup in a different typeface or change its letter-spacing.
- **Don't** place the navy-field mark on a busy photo — use the transparent or one-color version.
- **Don't** enlarge or "fix" the Λ to imply Λ-uniqueness is settled. It is **Conjecture 1
  (open)** and the mark draws it small and quiet on purpose.

## 7 · The marks, honestly

The orbital ellipse is the **khipu / receipt chain** rendered as a path you could fly through;
the coral node is **one claim** sitting on that chain. The Λ is the **Λ-Spine** aggregator
(weighted geometric mean — monotone A1, bounded A4); its unconditional uniqueness is
**Conjecture 1 (open)**, not a theorem, and the mark never implies otherwise. This is the brand
value: the logo claims exactly what is on disk, and no more.
